Local Food

In my hometown, the local food you can try is the following.

  • Krengsengan, a lamb dish
  • Pecel, a vegetable dish
  • Rawon, a beef dish
  • Rujak cingur, a beef dish
  • Sate, can be any kind of meat
  • Soto, such as Soto Lamongan, a chicken dish
  • Tahu campur, mixed tofu
  • etc.
